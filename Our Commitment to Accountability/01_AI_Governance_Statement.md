# Dëkkal — AI Governance Statement
**Public Commitment to Ethical AI | April 2026**

---

## Our Commitment

Dëkkal is committed to developing and deploying artificial intelligence that is transparent, accountable, and fair. Our flood risk scoring system is designed to support — not replace — human judgment in insurance underwriting decisions.

We operate under the following non-negotiable principles:

**1. Geophysical Data Only**
Our AI model uses exclusively satellite imagery, terrain elevation, rainfall statistics, soil properties, and drainage network data. No personal, demographic, income, or identity data is collected, stored, or processed at any stage.

**2. Human-in-the-Loop**
All scores above 75/100 require mandatory human review before any underwriting decision. No automated approval or denial of insurance coverage is permitted.

**3. Explainability by Design**
Every risk score is accompanied by a minimum of three ranked explanatory factors. No opaque score is ever delivered. Insurers can request a full feature attribution report for any individual score.

**4. Data Sovereignty**
All data is hosted at Sénégal Numérique SA — an ISO 27001 certified sovereign datacenter in Diamniadio, Senegal. No data transfer outside West Africa without explicit contractual consent.

**5. Continuous Accountability**
The model is recalibrated quarterly. Bias audits are conducted every quarter. An external audit is conducted annually. All audit results are available to clients upon request.
